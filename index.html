Vote Center Siting Tool

This user-friendly, web-based tool is intended to connect election officials to information sources that complement their own direct knowledge of their counties’ profiles and needs. To assist in identifying potential Vote Center and Vote-by-Mail drop box locations, the tool incorporates data representing the criteria required by the Voter's Choice Act. These data can be used to generate interactive web maps indicating optimal geographic areas for locating prospective Vote Centers and Vote-by-Mail drop boxes.

County Selection

Click on a county on the map or in the link below to be taken to the interactive web-based tool for that county. The counties were selected to be illustrative of the tool’s potential for decision support in making siting decisions reflective of SB 450.
